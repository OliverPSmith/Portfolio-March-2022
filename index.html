Bio

I’m a self taught web developer who takes pride in making websites with clean and professional code, as well as aesthetically pleasing designs and colour schemes. I also focus on making sure websites are straightforward to use, ensuring a site is as easy navigating around as it is on the eye.

As well as being a very prompt and punctual person I am also an excellent communicator, which is key when it comes to working with clients (check out my freelance work), and indeed as part of a team.

I learnt in the same way that many self taught programmers did, and as you can see in the programming page I mainly use the HTML, CSS and Javascript languages. However I am always branching out and learning new technologies - React being one that I am improving on currently.

Considering the amount of time I spend working on a computer I make sure to keep active, fit and engaged in my hobbies and social life. I play a lot of sport, read a lot of books, watch a lot of films, and, on the weekends - I may drink a lot of drinks. As they say, “balance is key”, so even though I may dedicate a lot of time to my craft I absolutely love what I do, and as each project becomes a passion project I delve deep into it.

Thanks for staying with me so far and for having a look at my portfolio. Make sure to check out both my freelance and programming work (and the code that has produced them) and let me know what you think.
